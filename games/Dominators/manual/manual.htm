Requirements:

A 3d graphics accelerator card.
A sound card if you need sound
A cpu running at 500 mhz or more

Instalation:



Extract the game to the desired directory and run the game (Dominators.exe).

Compilation:


This was developed using dev-c++ so it will be very easy for you to download and install this IDE rather than try to compile it with another IDE.
The compilation requires you to copy these folders found in NonStandardHeaders to your include directory boost, fmod, lua.
The game is made using a libary that one of our group members has been working on in his spare time.
It includes clases for texture management, sound, vectors, networking and other game related stuff. It is not as well commented as the game code because it was never intended to be read by other people :)
The libary must be compiled first before the game itself, the file projects.dev should be opened using dev-c++ to compile projects.a
The source specific to the game though can be found in "/projects/games/dominators"

Instructions:



Press fire to shoot, avoid the enmies and their projectiles while trying to destroy them with your own fire.
Move the NeoTank with the arrow keys on the keyboard.

GameControlls:


End to pause
Space to shoot
F11 to go to sound
F12 to go to full screen

Story:


A race of aliens called The Dominators is feared throughout the galaxy.
They love technology and love stealing it from other races even more.
The main defense/weapon used throughout the galaxy are neo-tanks,
these tanks can fire an array of different beam, laser or cannon fire and they
can also be upgraded to become even more powerful.
The game (Dominators) centers around the Dominators sending the newest recruit Adel for his initiation:
he must take over the planet Telos with his custom built neo-tank and steal their technology from their
Technology Centre.
The user controls Adel.
You must shoot the incoming aliens trying to defend their Tech Centre.
There will however be one boss after the first enemies are defeated.
He must be defeated to acquire the Telosians technology.
Power ups will also be used that can augment the abilities and artillery of Adel’s neo-tank.

Enemies:

The boss: More powerfull than any other tank.
Adel's tank (the main tank)
Enemy A: Fastest moving tank but slow on the draw
Enemy B: Second fastest tank, and good shooting speed as well these tanks tend to survive the longest
Enemy C: Not the worst when it comes to speed but makes up for it in its rapid fire.
Enemy D: Fastest shooter
